## 🔍 PHƯƠNG PHÁP LUẬN CHI TIẾT

### 🎯 **1. Xác định Market Bias**

**📊 Timeframe Analysis:**
- **H4/Daily:** Trend tổng thể
- **H1:** Entry timing  
- **M15/M5:** Fine-tuning entries

**🔍 Công cụ đánh giá:**
- EMA alignment
- Price action
- Support/Resistance levels

### 📈 **2. Setup Entry Signals**

**🟢 Long Setup:**
- Price above EMA 200
- EMA 34 > EMA 89 > EMA 200
- Pullback to EMA 34/89
- Bounce confirmation

**🔴 Short Setup:**  
- Price below EMA 200
- EMA 34 < EMA 89 < EMA 200
- Pullback to EMA 34/89
- Rejection confirmation

### 🎯 **3. Entry Execution**

**⚡ Entry Triggers:**
- Candlestick patterns
- Break of mini resistance/support
- Volume confirmation (nếu có)

**🛑 Stop Loss:**
- Below/above recent swing
- Beyond EMA levels
- Risk: 1-2% per trade

**🎯 Take Profit:**
- R:R ratio 1:2 hoặc 1:3  
- Key resistance/support levels
- EMA targets
